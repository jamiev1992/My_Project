Results of three Personality Tests

I took three tests online which were Myers-Briggs test, Vark Learning Test and Creative Test.

Results of the Myers-Briggs Test

Mediator: poetic, kind, altruistic and eager to help a good cause

Mind: 27% - Extraverted and 73% Introverted – prefer fewer, deep, meaningful social interactions and feel drawn to calmer environments

Energy: 69% Intuitive and 31% Observant – imaginative, open-minded, curious, value originality, focus on hidden meanings and distant possibilities

Nature: 34% Thinking and 66% Feeling – value emotional expression, sensitivity, lot of importance on empathy, social harmony and cooperation

Tactics: 35% Judging and 65% Prospecting – very good at improvising, adapting to opportunities, flexible nonconformists who value novelty above stability

Identity – 22% Assertive and 78% Turbulent – self-conscious and sensitive to stress, feel a sense of urgency in emotions and tend to be success-driven, perfectionistic and eager to improve

Vark Learning Test

Multimodal – prefer different formats like graphs, diagrams, maps, interesting layouts, space, listening, discussing, talking, questioning, notes, handouts, print, text, practical exercises, experiences, examples, case studies, trail, error and things that are real

Creative Test

What the reults meant to me

The results of the tests mean to me is that I am creative type thinker and I need to be able to interact to help with my learning style. This will affect me as I don’t like to take charge, I prefer to follow and take instructions. I would consider that I am shy and consider what I am assigned to do and make sure I do it without letting my team down.
